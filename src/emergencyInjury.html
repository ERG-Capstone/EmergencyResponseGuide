Injury Emergency


  • For all 'serious' injuries, please immediately call 911
    • Call 911

  • To obtain medical attention at Campus Health by campus (See Emergency Contacts).


    • For heart-related injuries


    Do the following:

    • Get victim to cool place.

    • Loosen tight clothing.

    • Apply cool, wet cloths to the skin.

    • Fan the victim.

    • If conscious, give cool water to drink.

    • If conscious, give cool water to drink.

    • Call 911 for a anmbulance if victim refuses water, vomits or loses consciousness.

    For minor cut or puncture injuries


    Do the following:

    • Vigorously wash injury with soap and water for serveral minutes.

    • If impaled by foreign object DO NOT remove the object. Seek medical attention immediately.

    For material in eye


    Do the following:

    • Immediately rinse eye with water continuously for 15 minutes.

    • Forcably hold eye open while washing.
