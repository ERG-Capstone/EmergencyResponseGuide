Power Outage


  • Remain calm and don't panic

  • Locate flashlight

  • Report minor outages to Facilities Management

  • For major outages, follow instructions of ASU Police

  • Open windows/doors for ventilation

  • Unplug/turn off electronics

  • Secure belongings

Quick tips:

  • Walk, do not run
  • Do NOT use elevators
  • Gather outside at a designated staging area and wait for your supervisor to take roll
  • Do NOT re-enter the building unless it is instructed from ASU police or other local law enforcement agency
