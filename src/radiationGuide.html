Radiation Spill

General

  • Radioactive material will usually be limited to minor spills.

  • The potential for significant radiation hazards does exist.

  • Radiation incidents may be complicated by injuries to personnel.

    • PRIMARY CONCERN - protection of personnel from radiation and non-radiation hazards.

    SECONDARY CONCERN - confine contamination.

  • Medical assistance should not be withheld or delayed because of contamination of personnel by radioactive material.

  • Permit no one to resume work in the area or leave the premises without approval of the Radiation Safety Officer (RSO) or his/her designated representative.

  • Notify Office of Radiation Safety (Mon-Fri, 8am-5pm: 480.965.6140) immediately following an incident.

Spills

  • Notify all other persons in the room at once.

  • Confine the spill immediately, but make no attempt to clean up the spill.

  • For liquid spills, don protective gloves and place absorbent paper on the spill.

  • For dry spills, don protective gloves and place lightly moistened absorbent paper on the spill, taking care not to spread contamination.

  • If the spill is on the skin, flush thoroughly with water.

  • If the spill is on the clothing, discard outer clothing at once.

Incidents Involving Radioactive Substances

  • Notify all personnel to vacate the room immediately.

  • Hold breath, close all windows and escape valves, and switch off circulating air.

  • Vacate the room.

  • Notify Office of Radiation Safety (Mon-Fri, 8am-5pm: 480.965.6140) immediately following an incident.

  • Ascertain that all doors giving access to the room are closed and locked.

  • If necessary, post guards to prevent accidental opening of doors.

Injuries to Personnel Involving Radiation Hazards

  • Wash minor wounds immediately under running water.

  • Report all radiation accidents (wound, over exposure, ingestion, and inhalation) to the ORS immediately.

  • Obtain medical attention at the Campus Heath by campus (See Emergency Contacts section).

  • Do not permit personnel involved in a radiation injury incident to return to work or leave the premises without approval of the RSO or physician.

Spill on the Body
  • Remove/bag contaminated clothing.

  • Rinse exposed area thoroughly with water.

  • Obtain medical attention.

Burns
  • Shut off machine - DO NOT alter machine configuration until RSO inspection.

  • Obtain medical attention.
