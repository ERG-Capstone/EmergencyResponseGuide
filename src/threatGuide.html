Personal Threat

Stalking

  • Call 911 and seek the safety of others.

  • Do not confront alleged stalker.

  • Take note of any identifying features to report later to police.

Assault

  • Call 911.

  • Remain with victim until help arrives.

Relationship Violence

  • Call 911 for medical emergencies or immediate threats.

    • Support Resource Information

  • ASU students at ASU Counseling Services.

  • ASU Employees at ASU Employee Assistance Office.

  • The ASU 24 hour dedicated crisis line: EMPACT 480.921.1006

Civil Disturbance

  • Civil disturbances include riots, demonstrations, threatening individuals, crimes in progress, or assemblies that have become significantly disruptive.

  • Call 911.

  • Avoid provoking or obstructing demonstrators.

  • Secure your area (lock doors, safes, files, vital records, etc.).

  • Avoid area of disturbance.

  • Continue with normal routines if possible.

  • If the disturbance is outside, stay away from doors/windows. STAY INSIDE!

  • Prepare for evacuation or relocation.
