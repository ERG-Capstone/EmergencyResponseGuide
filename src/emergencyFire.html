    Fire Extinguisher Quick Tips


    Step 1


    Pull the pin from the top of the fire extinguisher



    Step 2


    Aim the nozzle at the fire



    Step 3


    Squeeze the handle to discharge the agent on the fire



    Step 4


    Sweep the nozzle left-to-right at the base of the fire and continue discharging a few seconds after all flames are gone.
