Flood Emergency

General

  • Warn others of the hazard.

  • Building occupants should immediately report flooding or incidents of standing water to the ASU Police or local law enforcement agency. They will dispatch the appropriate departments for assistance.

  • Secure vital equipment, records, and chemicals (move to higher, safer ground). Shut off all electrical equipment. Secure all laboratory experiments.

  • Use extreme caution around appliances or outlets near the leak and/or water.

  • Avoid puddles and wet areas as the floor may be slick.

  • Follow instructions of first responders regarding building evacuation.

  • Notify emergency personnel if others are still inside the building.
