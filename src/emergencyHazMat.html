    Chemical Spill on Body


    • Flood exposed area with running water.

    • Remove contaminated clothing and place into plastic bag.

    • Ensure no contamination on shoes, jewelry, etc.

    • Check Materials Safety Data Sheets for possible effects.

    • Report incident to supervisor. Supervisor responsible for notifying ASU EH&S.

    • Contact ASU EH&S for personnel trained in proper clean-up and containment (See Emergency Contacts).

    Minor Spill/Leak


    • Alert those in immediate area.

    • Wear protective equipement and attempt to clean up.

    • Avoid breathing vapors, try to confine the spill to a small area.

    • DO NOT allow anything to discharge into drains.

    • Bag and label cleanup material with Hazardous Waste Label.

    • Report incident to supervisor. Supervisor responsible for notifying ASU EH&S.

    • Contact ASU EH&S for personnel trained in proper clean-up and containment (See Emergency Contacts).
