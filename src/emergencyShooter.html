Active Shooter Emergency


Run

  • If there is an escape path, attempt to evactuate.

  • Evacuate whether others agree to or not.

  • Leave your belongings behind.

Hide

  • Lock and/or block the door.

  • Silence your cell phone.

  • Remain quiet.

  • Your hiding place should be:

    • Be out of shooters view

    • Provide protection if shots are fired in your direction

    • Do not trap or restrict your options for movement.

Fight

  • Incappacitate the shooter by any means necessary.

  • Act with physical agression.

  • Improvise weapons.

  • Commit to your actions.

When Police Arrive

  • Follow instructions.

  • Keep hands visible at all times.

  • Avoid pointing or yelling.
