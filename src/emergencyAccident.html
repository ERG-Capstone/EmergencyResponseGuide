    Vehicle Accident


    Do the following:

    • Remain on the scene until released by police.

    • Advise operator of injuries and/or hazardous conditions.

    • DO NOT attempt to move victims unless more dangerous to leave them.

    • Attempt to calm victims, let them know help is on the way.

    Aircraft Accident


    Do the following:

    • Notify operator of accident location, nature of injuries and/or affected area.

    • If close to your building, notify supervisor and evacuate if you feel you are in danger.

    • DO NOT approach a downed aircraft.

    • Avoid smoke from wreckage as it is very toxic.

    • Attempt to calm victims, let them know help is on the way.
