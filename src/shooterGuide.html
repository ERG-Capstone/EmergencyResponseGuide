Active Shooter

General

  • An active shooter is defined as an individual who is actively engaged in killing or attempting to kill people.

  • In most cases active shooters use a firearm(s) but in some cases weapons other than a firearm, including explosive devices may be used.

  • When an active shooter is in your vicinity: run, hide, and/or fight!

Run

  • If there is an escape path, attempt to evacuate.

  • Evacuate whether others agree to or not

  • Leave your belongings behind

Hide

  • Lock and/or block the door.

  • Silence your cell phone.

  • Remain quiet.

  • Hiding place should:

    • Be out of the shooter's view.

    • Provide protection if shots are fired in your direction.

    • Do not trap or restrict your options for movement.


Fight

  • Incapacitate the shooter by any means possible.

  • Act with physical aggression.

  • Improvise weapons.

  • Commit to your actions.

When Law Enforcement Arrives

  • Follow instructions.

  • Keep your hands visible at all times.

  • Avoid pointing or yelling.

  • Know that help for the injured is on the way.
