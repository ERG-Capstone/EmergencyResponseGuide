    Spill Emergency


    • Notify all persons in the room

    • Confine spill immediately

    • Liquid spills - Wear protective gloves and place absorbant paper on spill

    • Dry spills - Wear protective gloves and place absorbant paper on spill

    • Spill on skin - Flush thoroughly with water

    • Spill on clothing - Discard clothing at once

    Fumes/Vapor Emergency


    • Notify all personnel to vacaqte the room immediately.

    • Hold breath, close all windows and escape valves and switch off circulating air.

    • Vacate the room.

    • Notify the Office of Radiation Safety (M-F 8am-5pm, 480.965.6140) immediately following the incident.

    • Assure all doors with access to the room are closed and locked.

    • If necessary, post guards to prevent accidental opening of doors.

    Injuries


    • Wash minor wounds immediately under running water.

    • Report all radiation accidents (wounds, over exposure, injestion or inhalation) to ORS (M-F 8am-5pm, 480.965.6140) immediately.

    • Obtain medical attention at Campus Heath by campus (see Emergency Contacts for campus).

    • DO NOT permit personnel involved in injury to return to work or leave premises without approval of RSO or physician.
